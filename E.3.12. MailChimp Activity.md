<h2 id="use-of-mailchimp-in-aira" class="toc-header">Use of Mailchimp in AIRA:</h2>
<ul>
 	<li>Integration with Mailchimp in AIRA enables users to automate email marketing tasks within their workflows.</li>
 	<li>Users can create and manage email campaigns directly from the AIRA platform, streamlining their marketing processes.</li>
 	<li>By leveraging Mailchimp's features, such as audience segmentation and email personalization, users can enhance the effectiveness of their email marketing efforts.</li>
 	<li>Integration with Mailchimp allows for seamless communication with subscribers and customers, helping businesses stay connected and engaged with their audience.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="create-a-campaign-app" class="toc-header">Create a Campaign App:</h2>
The "Create a Campaign" app in AIRA Mailchimp enables users to set up and launch email campaigns directly from the AIRA platform.
<h3 id="how-to-use-the-app" class="toc-header">How to Use the App:</h3>
<ul>
 	<li>Navigate to the Mailchimp app in the AIRA app section and select "Create a Campaign" app.</li>
 	<li>Drag and drop the app into the AIRA workflow designer and connect it to the desired location.</li>
 	<li>Double-click on the app to access its settings.</li>
 	<li>Select the required connection to access Mailchimp. If Connection is not available click on add button to create a new connection. To know more about connections visit AIRA <a class="is-external-link" href="https://wiki.aira.fr/docs/connections/">Connections</a>.</li>
 	<li>Enter the campaign title, which can be a static text or dynamically populated using variables.</li>
 	<li>Choose the audience (list ID) to which the campaign will be sent, available in the connection.</li>
 	<li>Enter the subject line of the email campaign, using variables if needed.</li>
 	<li>Provide preview text for the email, which appears alongside the subject line in recipients' inboxes using variables if needed.</li>
 	<li>Enter the sender's name and email address, which can be dynamic using variables.</li>
 	<li>Specify the reply-to email address and name for recipients' responses.</li>
 	<li>Select the Folder ID where the campaign will be stored in Mailchimp.</li>
 	<li>Enter the template ID for the email campaign, available in the Mailchimp connection.</li>
 	<li>Click the submit button to save the settings and create the campaign in Mailchimp.</li>
</ul>
<strong>Additional Options:</strong>

Right-click on the app in the AIRA Designer workflow to access additional options:
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Unlink:</strong> Disconnect the app from any connected apps or tools in the workflow.</li>
 	<li><strong>Setting:</strong> Access and modify the settings of the app.</li>
 	<li><strong>Rename:</strong> Change the name of the apps for better organization.</li>
 	<li><strong>Trigger:</strong> Set triggers on the app to initiate actions based on specified conditions or events.</li>
 	<li><strong>Delete Module:</strong> Permanently delete the app from the workflow if it's no longer needed.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="edit-a-template-content-app" class="toc-header">Edit a Template Content App:</h2>
The "Edit a Template Content" app in AIRA Mailchimp allows users to customise the content of email templates directly within the AIRA platform. This feature streamlines the process of editing email templates and ensures seamless integration with Mailchimp's email marketing campaigns.

<ul>

 	<li>Drag and drop the app into the AIRA workflow designer and connect it to the desired location within the workflow.</li>
 	<li>Double-click on the app to access its settings and configure the editing process.</li>
 	<li>Choose the required connection to access Mailchimp, ensuring seamless communication between AIRA and Mailchimp. If Connection is not available click on add button to create a new connection. To know more about connections visit AIRA <a class="is-external-link" href="https://wiki.aira.fr/docs/connections/">Connections</a>.</li>
 	<li>Select the template you wish to edit from the available options within the AIRA Mailchimp connection.</li>
 	<li>Provide the campaign ID associated with the template. This information can be static or dynamic, using variables to populate the details.</li>
