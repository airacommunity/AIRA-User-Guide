## Overview
Welcome to the user guide for utilising ML models in the AIRA workflow. This guide will walk you through the process of incorporating ML models into your AIRA design seamlessly.

<br>

> <img align="left" width="40" height="40" src="https://github.com/airacommunity/AIRA-User-Guide-Images/blob/main/Icon-Warning.png?raw=true"> Kindly note that the content provided is subject to regular updates. It may not reflect the final version. Your understanding is appreciated.

<br>

### Accessing ML Models in AIRA:


**1. Navigate to AIRA ML Models:** 
- Open the AIRA application and locate the ML Models section within the app section.

**2. Select Desired Model:** 
- Click on ML Models to access a list of available models developed within the AIRA ecosystem.

**3. Drag and Drop into AIRA Designer:** 
- Choose the ML model you wish to use and simply drag and drop it into the AIRA designer interface.

**4. Connect to Workflow:** 
- Once the ML model is placed within the AIRA designer, connect it to the relevant components of your workflow by establishing connections as needed.

**Additional Options:**

Right-click on the app in the AIRA Designer workflow to access additional options:

- Unlink: Disconnect the app from any connected apps or tools in the workflow.
- Setting: Access and modify the settings of the app.
- Rename: Change the name of the apps for better organization.
- Trigger: Set triggers on the app to initiate actions based on specified conditions or events.
- Delete Module: Permanently delete the app from the workflow if it's no longer needed.
